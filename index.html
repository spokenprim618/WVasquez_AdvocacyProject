What is Emotional Intelligence

Emotional intelligence is the ability to understand and manage your own emotions, as well as the emotions of others.

Four Attributes

Self Management

In emotional intelligence, self-management refers to the ability to regulate one's emotions, thoughts, and behaviors effectively.

Self Awareness

Self-awareness in emotional intelligence involves recognizing and understanding one's own emotions and how they affect thoughts and behavior.

Social Awareness

Social awareness is the capacity to understand and empathize with others, recognizing their emotions and perspectives.

Relationship Management

Relationship management refers to the ability to develop and maintain healthy relationships by effectively managing emotions and communication.

Why is it important

Emotional intelligence is important because it helps individuals understand and manage emotions effectively.

Affects

Performance at school or work

Emotional intelligence can positively impact performance in academic settings or professional environments by enhancing interpersonal relationships and decision-making.

Mental health and Physical health

Emotional intelligence plays a crucial role in maintaining mental health and physical well-being by promoting self-awareness, stress management, and resilience.

Relationships

Strong emotional intelligence skills contribute to building and nurturing meaningful relationships based on trust, empathy, and effective communication.

Social intelligence

Social intelligence encompasses emotional intelligence and adds the ability to navigate complex social situations, understand social dynamics, and build social connections effectively.
